About Me

Hello! My name is Adika Rahman, a 21-year-old student currently studying at Universitas Esa Unggul. As a passionate and dedicated web developer, I have had the opportunity to work on various projects that have helped me hone my skills and gain valuable experience in the field.

During my time at the university, I participated in the MSIB Micro and Massive projects where I took on the role of a web designer. I successfully designed and developed two significant projects: "Food Recipe," a website for sharing recipes, and "Wedding Organizer," a platform to help couples plan their weddings. These experiences have allowed me to develop a strong foundation in web design and user experience (UI/UX).

I'm eager to continue learning and growing as a web developer, taking on new challenges, and contributing to exciting projects. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see more of my work, and don't hesitate to contact me if you'd like to collaborate!
